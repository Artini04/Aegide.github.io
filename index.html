Infinite Fusion Calculator

Only works with natives mons available in Pokémon Infinite Fusion v5 ! This project was created by SDM0, then maintained by Aegide. The data used is from the game, but is mostly based on generation 7. Custom sprites in green are made by artists, from the Infinite Fusion Discord and Reddit. Generated sprites in red are from Japeal. They were extracted and fixed by Aegide.
